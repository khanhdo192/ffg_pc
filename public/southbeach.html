The mega South Beach complex is a joint venture between City Development Limited (CDL) and IOI Properties. Strategically located on Beach Road bordering Singapore’s CBD, the mixed-use development seamlessly integrated four conserved military buildings with 2 towers to collectively comprise 510,000 sqft of Grade A office space, 190 luxury residences, the 634-room JW Marriott Singapore South Beach and an array of dining and lifestyle establishments at South Beach Avenue.


Conceived by world renowned architectural firm Foster + Partners, South Beach is designed to create a distinctive, high-quality development that fits in with Singapore’s tropical climate and urban context. Its state-of-the-art microclimatic canopy brilliantly showcases form and function with its iconic design and ecological features which include harvesting rainwater to support irrigation and photovoltaic panels which convert solar energy to help power the facade's lighting.


Blending innovative form with environmentally conscious design and ingenious green technologies, South Beach has been honoured with two Green Mark Platinum Awards (Residential and Commercial). It also received the prestigious FIABCI Singapore Awards, topping the Sustainable Development category and also conferred the highest industry honour of 5-stars in the Best Mixed-Use Development (Singapore) category at the Asia Pacific Property Awards.
